Frequently Asked Questions

How do I extract a recipe from a photo?
Simply upload a photo of your recipe using the app. Our AI will automatically extract the ingredients, instructions, and other details from the image.
Can I import recipes from websites?
Yes! You can paste a link from any recipe website, and we'll extract the recipe information for you automatically.
How do I organize my recipes?
You can create color-coded folders (similar to Pinterest boards) and organize your recipes by theme, cuisine, or any category you prefer.
Is my data secure?
Yes, we take your privacy seriously. Your recipes and personal information are stored securely. Please see our Privacy Policy for more details.
Can I use the app in multiple languages?
Yes! Our AI understands and can extract recipes from multiple languages. You can also create and view recipes in any language.
